1Receiving Requirements

Shipping Labels & ASN# Requirements

  • Each shipping label associated with a receiving must include a properly formatted shipping address and a unique identifier assigned to inbound inventory shipments, which must be submitted through Handled's Warehouse Management System (WMS) before inventory is shipped to Handled ("Advanced Shipping Notification Number" or "ASN#").
  • Each individual parcel, pallet, and container ("piece") must be legibly and durably labeled with the shipping address and ASN#.
  • Each tracking number must reference only one ASN# (though an ASN# may have multiple tracking numbers).
  • Customer must submit the ASN# directly into the Handled system through the WMS prior to shipment.
  • For freight shipments (non-parcel), Customer must also provide a digital bill of lading through the Handled WMS prior to shipment.

ASN# Age & Quantity Matching

  • All ASN#s must be less than three weeks (15 business days) old.
  • The actual quantities arriving at the warehouse must match the reported quantities in the ASN#.
  • The received product dimensions and weights must match those reported in the Handled account.

Scheduling & Delivery

  • If Customer sends eight (8) or more pallets to Handled under one ASN#, Customer must schedule a delivery window before the inventory arrives at the warehouse.

Packaging & Labeling Compliance

  • Inventory must be properly packaged with appropriate labels.
  • Each individual item must have readable Code 128 barcode labels corresponding to the Inventory ID (SKU) in the Handled account.
  • A standard receiving involves inventory that is correctly labeled, palletized, and marked before arrival at the warehouse.
  • All shipments must comply with applicable local, state, and federal laws, including those governing packaging, marking, and labeling.
  • Customer must comply with all labeling guidelines before sending inventory to Handled.

Inventory Condition & Organization

  • Inventory must be cleanly organized, boxed, and/or stacked upon arrival.

2Importer of Record & Customs Compliance

Duties, Fees, & Customs Responsibilities

  • Handled cannot act as the Importer of Record for Customer's merchandise.
  • Handled is not liable for any duties, customs fees, or other charges related to the inventory.
  • Shipments must be free and clear of all duties and fees before arriving at Handled.
  • Customer must be the ultimate consignee and take full responsibility for customs clearance, inspections, and regulatory compliance.
  • Customer must have all requisite tax IDs and business registrations as required by local customs authorities at the destination warehouse.

Customs Fees & Holds

  • Handled reserves the right to refuse any shipment that arrives with unpaid customs duties, fees, or other charges.
  • If merchandise arrives at Handled with unpaid customs duties, fees, or related charges, these fees will be billed to the Customer's Account along with a processing fee as set forth in Handled's Standard Service Fees.
  • Inventory may be held from outbound shipment until all applicable fees are paid.
  • If additional duties or fees are assessed at a later date, penalties and holds may be applied retroactively and immediately.

3Palletized Inventory Requirements

Weight & Size Restrictions

Requirement Specification
Pallet footprint 40" × 48" standard
Maximum pallet height 55 inches (including skid)
Maximum pallet weight 2,000 lbs (including skid)
Parcel requiring palletization Any carton/master pack exceeding 70 lbs or with combined length and girth greater than 120 inches
  • The skid or base weight is included in the total weight limit and must be provided by Customer. Additional charges may apply if Handled provides a pallet or skid, as set forth in Handled's Standard Service Fees.
  • Handled reserves the right to refuse shipment on non-compliant receipts or to re-configure non-compliant pallets. Additional charges may apply for non-compliant pallets as set forth in Handled's Standard Service Fees.
Non-compliance fees apply to any receipt that does not meet these requirements and requires corrective measures by Handled. See Standard Service Fees for current rates.
