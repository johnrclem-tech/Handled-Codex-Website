Data Processing
Agreement

Date of Last Revision: September 24, 2025

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") is part of the Agreement between the Customer and Handled, Inc. ("Handled," "we," "us," or "our") and governs our processing of Personal Data on behalf of the Customer in connection with the Services. This DPA is incorporated into and forms part of the Handled Terms of Service.

1Definitions

"Applicable Data Protection Laws" means all laws and regulations governing the use, processing, and protection of Personal Data, including the GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA/CPRA.

"Personal Data" means any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual that Handled processes on behalf of Customer.

"Process," "Controller," "Processor," "Data Subject," and "Subprocessor" have the meanings given under Applicable Data Protection Laws.

2Roles and Scope

Customer is the Controller of Personal Data. Handled is the Processor, processing Personal Data solely to provide the Services. Each party will comply with the obligations applicable to it under Applicable Data Protection Laws.

3Purpose and Nature of Processing

Handled processes Personal Data solely as necessary to provide the Services as directed by Customer. The scope of processing under this DPA is limited to:

  • Fulfillment, warehousing, and logistics operations required to deliver the Services
  • Account management, customer support, and related communications
  • Maintenance, security, and improvement of the Services, including troubleshooting, performance monitoring, and development of new features or offerings
  • Compliance with applicable legal obligations

Handled does not process Personal Data for its own independent commercial purposes beyond the scope described above.

4Customer Responsibilities

Customer represents and warrants that it has all necessary rights, consents, and legal bases to provide Personal Data to Handled and to authorize its processing. Customer is solely responsible for the accuracy, quality, legality, and adequacy of Personal Data.

5Handled's Processing Obligations

Within the scope described in Section 3, Handled commits to the following:

  • Process only as instructed. Handled will process Personal Data only as necessary to provide the Services and as otherwise directed by Customer in writing, except where processing is required by Applicable Data Protection Laws.
  • No sale or sharing. Handled will not sell or share Personal Data as those terms are defined under CCPA/CPRA. Any disclosure to subprocessors or service providers is made solely to support delivery of the Services for the business purposes authorized by Customer.
  • Technical and organizational safeguards. Handled will implement and maintain commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect Personal Data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure, as further described in Section 8.
  • Access controls. Handled will ensure that access to Personal Data is limited to authorized individuals who are subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations.
  • Breach notification. Handled will notify Customer of any confirmed Personal Data breach without undue delay, and in any event consistent with Handled's notification obligations under the Terms of Service. Notification will include, to the extent known: the nature of the breach, the categories and approximate number of individuals and records affected, likely consequences, and measures taken or proposed to address the breach.
  • Data subject assistance. Handled may, at its discretion, assist Customer in responding to verified data subject requests within a commercially reasonable timeframe. Customer remains solely responsible for responding to data subjects directly.

6Subprocessors

Handled may use third-party service providers to support delivery of the Services. These may include WMS platforms, cloud infrastructure providers, analytics tools, CRM systems, and shipping carriers. All such subprocessors are engaged solely to support service delivery under obligations consistent with this DPA.

Handled remains responsible to Customer for the performance of subprocessors' obligations under this DPA to the extent required by Applicable Data Protection Laws. General information about Handled's privacy and security practices is available in the Privacy Policy.

7International Transfers

Handled primarily processes Personal Data in the United States. Handled may make available additional data transfer mechanisms, such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, UK addenda, or Swiss addenda, if required by law and agreed upon in writing by the parties.

8Security Measures

Handled maintains commercially reasonable safeguards designed to protect Personal Data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Secure physical and digital storage
  • Internal data handling policies

General information about Handled's privacy and security practices is available in the Privacy Policy and Section 12(d) of the Terms of Service. To report a suspected security incident, contact security@handledcommerce.com.

9Data Retention

Handled retains Personal Data for the periods set forth in the Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law or the Agreement. Upon termination of the Agreement, Handled will delete or anonymize Personal Data within a reasonable period consistent with its retention schedule, unless retention is required by law.

10Audit Rights

Handled may, once per year and upon written request, provide documentation or a summary of its data protection practices. Any broader audit right must be expressly required by law and pre-approved in writing by Handled. Customer agrees to bear the cost of any such audit. General information about Handled's privacy and security practices is available in the Privacy Policy; Customer is encouraged to review that document before submitting a formal audit request.

11Liability

The limitation of liability under this DPA shall be governed by the limitation set forth in the Agreement. Nothing in this DPA increases or expands the liability cap agreed to in the underlying Agreement.

12General

This DPA is governed by the same law and jurisdiction as the Agreement. If any provision is found unenforceable, the remainder shall remain in effect.

In case of conflict between this DPA and the Privacy Policy, this DPA shall control. In case of conflict between this DPA and the Terms of Service or any Order Form, the Terms of Service or Order Form shall control.

13Contact

For questions about this DPA or privacy-related issues, please contact legal@handledcommerce.com.
